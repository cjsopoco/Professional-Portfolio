About me

My name is Christian Sopoco, I also go by Chris. I was born in the Philippines but raised in Dallas and now living in Seattle. I studied Merchandising and Marketing in college and now work for Target as a Merchandise planner in their corporate office. Outside of work I like to workout, try new foods, and travel as much as I can.

Growing up technology played a big role in my life. Whether it was chatting with my relatives overseas or even using my computer to help me with my homework because my parents didn't understand, I knew I could count on technology to get me what I wanted and needed. I didn't always have the right resources but I knew I could count on technology for the solution. Currently I know there are ways technology can open a new world for retail. As an individual contributer in the world of retail I see countless opportunites to leverage technology and build a bridge that's going to connect the fundamentals or retail with the ingenuity of tech. This is my body of work.
